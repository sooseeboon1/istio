Local istio setup
1) 2 namespaces for istio
   a) istio-system <-- kiali/grafana service etc..
   b) istio-ingress <-- Gateway pod, gateway service, etc..

2) kubectl port-forward service/ssootest-aaclient 8088:8080  <-- Port forward to my service
   kubectl port-forward service/kiali 20001:20001 -n istio-system <-- Port forward to kiali
   kubectl port-forward service/istio-ingressgateway 8888:80 -n istio-ingress <-- Port forward to istio gateway (istio gateway is listen on port 80)
 
3) To test aaclient: Send http get to: http://localhost:8888/hello
